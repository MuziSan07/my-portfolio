Who I Am

I'm Ali Basit, an AI and full-stack engineer based in Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan. I build software that uses machine learning in a practical way, from training and deploying models to wiring them into web apps that people actually use.

Most of my recent work sits around large language models: chat agents, automation bots, and tools that generate or clean up content. I like taking a project the whole way, from a rough idea and a blank repo to something running in production that a client depends on.

I'm currently finishing my BS in Computer Science at Virtual University of Pakistan, and I take on freelance work through Fiverr and Upwork alongside it.
